About Smart Lender

Why Smart Lender?

Smart Lender is an AI-powered web application developed to assist banks and financial institutions in evaluating loan applications quickly and accurately. The application analyzes applicant information such as income, education, employment status, loan amount, credit history and property area using advanced Machine Learning algorithms.


The objective is to reduce manual verification time while increasing prediction accuracy and helping financial institutions make better loan approval decisions.
